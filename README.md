# 🚚 Supply Chain & Logistics Analytics

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing supply chain and logistics operations using Excel, SQL, and Power BI.

The objective is to monitor logistics KPIs, identify delivery delays, evaluate warehouse performance, and generate business insights for improving operational efficiency.

---

## 🛠 Tools Used

- Excel
- Power BI
- Pivot Tables
- DAX

---

## 📊 Dataset Information

- Total Orders: 10,000
- Multiple Cities across India
- Multiple Warehouses
- Multiple Delivery Partners
- Product Categories
- Delivery Status (On Time / Delayed)

## 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- Total Orders
- Total Revenue
- Average Shipping Cost
- On-Time Delivery Percentage

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 1. Warehouse Performance

- Compared warehouses based on total orders and revenue.
- Identified top-performing and low-performing warehouses.

### 2. City-wise Delivery Delays

- Analyzed delayed deliveries across cities.
- Identified cities with the highest delivery delays.

### 3. Delivery Partner Performance

- Compared courier partners based on delivery time and delays.
- Identified the most reliable and slowest delivery partners.

### 4. Product Category Revenue

- Evaluated revenue contribution from different product categories.
- Identified top revenue-generating categories.

---

## 💼 Business Recommendations

- Improve warehouse efficiency in underperforming regions.
- Optimize delivery routes in cities with high delays.
- Partner more with reliable delivery providers.
- Focus marketing efforts on high revenue product categories.
